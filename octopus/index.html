What is it?

Cybercriminals are actively using the Internet infrastructure to conduct attacks and hide their activity.

Therefore, security teams need a tool that would allow time to reverse and analyze the state of the Internet at a certain point in time to expose cybercriminals' attempts to hide their activity through changes in IP addresses, domains and name servers.

Security analysts and experts responsible for incidents need access to real-time data and historical Passive DNS data to protect their systems from attack and use by cybercriminals. Historical Passive DNS data also allows security teams to detect patterns of malicious activity, identify phishing or other targeted attacks.
